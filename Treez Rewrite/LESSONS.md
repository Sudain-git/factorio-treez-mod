# Lessons Learned

A running record of mistakes made during this rewrite, why they happened, and the rule to follow going forward. Read this at the start of every session. Add entries at the top so the most recent mistakes are seen first.

When a lesson is general enough to always apply, also add it to the relevant section of `CLAUDE.md`. If it is specific to one incident, keep it here only.

---

## How to Add an Entry

```
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Area:** one of: data-stage | control-stage | entity-layer | performance | lua | factorio-api | architecture
**What happened:** One or two sentences describing the concrete mistake.
**Why it happened:** The root cause — what assumption was wrong or what was overlooked.
**Rule going forward:** A specific, actionable rule that prevents the same mistake.
```
